WHY CHOOSE PROJECT GAIA?

Project GAIA simplifies the way Baguio rents.
We’re building a trusted space where style meets convenience — digitizing the city’s fragmented rental scene and bringing formal, cultural, and specialty wear to your fingertips.

Curated for Style & Occasion

Every piece is selected with intention — from sleek tuxedos to heritage garments — so you can rent confidently for any event, big or small.

Affordable. Flexible. Smart.

Why buy when you can rent smarter? Save money, skip the hassle, and access quality outfits exactly when you need them.

Rooted in the Local Community

We spotlight Baguio’s rental owners and tailors, empowering local fashion and making their collections visible, bookable, and accessible — all in one place.

About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of 3 people who purposefully created a website dedicated for renting or buying couture clothing in Baguio City.

With a focus on quality content and a user-friendly interface, we aim to empower rental shops and individuals looking for a good attire to rent.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the renting experience where you can browse hundreds of attires for rent in just your device and to ensure a smooth transaction between rental shops and their clients.
